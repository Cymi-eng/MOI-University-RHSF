About Our Fellowship

Moi University Main Altar

A Christ-centered fellowship committed to worship, prayer, discipleship, evangelism, and preparing the way for the coming of the Messiah.

Who We Are

Moi University Main Altar is a vibrant Christian fellowship dedicated to nurturing believers in holiness, righteousness, and faithful service to God. Through worship, prayer, Bible study, outreach, and fellowship, we seek to build a strong community of believers committed to living according to God's Word.

We welcome students, staff, and visitors from all backgrounds to join us as we grow spiritually and prepare for the glorious coming of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To raise a generation that walks in holiness, righteousness, and unwavering faith in preparation for the Kingdom of God.

Our Mission

To preach the Gospel, disciple believers, foster prayer and worship, and transform lives through the power of Jesus Christ.
